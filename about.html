FROM THE FOUNDER'S DESK

I started KARYA DESIGNS after trying my hand as a junior designer under an experienced architect, where I got to show my designing skills to the fullest but there was always something missing from my concepts. Maybe because I was being told what to design or how to design. That’s where my skills clashed. I needed a more open ground to get my skill set flowing to the fullest. It was like I wanted to contribute more to this profession. That’s when I decided to work on my own and see where it takes me. I explored a different side of me on this journey, I saw my creativity flowing more than ever. I wanted to get another outlook to the word bespoke and minimalism through my design.
