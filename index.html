Our vision reflects our values: integrity, service, excellence and teamwork.


Started in 2021, KARYA DESIGNS is an all services architecture and interior design firm based in Pune. Specializing in residential and commercial architecture and interiors, our design process focuses on the relationship every individual shares with their space. Enriching this relationship through concepts, tendering, design management and construction drawings, KARYA creates value and adds emotion to your space, to craft a bespoke and minimalistic design.
